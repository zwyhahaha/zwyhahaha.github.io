I'm currently a junior student at School of Information Management and Engineering (SIME), Shanghai University of Finance and Economics (SUFE). I'm broadly interested in topics in large-scale optimization, online algorithms, and optimization techniques for AI training.

Here are some research topics I've engaged in

  • DRL and Inventory Management: Multi-Agent Deep Reinforcement Learning for Decentralized Proactive Transshipment. with Jiayu Xu, Qiushi Han, Haoyue Tang, Prof. Chaolin Yang.

  • Optimization and Vision-based Localization: A Robust Anchor-based Method for Multi-Camera Pedestrian Localization. with Jiaqi Zhang, Prof. Huiking Liu, Prof. Yinyu Ye.

  • Manifold Optimization in Medical Imaging: Orientation Determination in Cryo-EM Images Using Block Stochastic Riemannian Subgradient Methods. with Prof.Huikang Liu.

  • First-Order-Method for QP: Restarted Primal-Dual Hybrid Conjugate Gradient Method for Large-Scale Quadratic Programming. with Yicheng Huang, Prof. Huiking Liu, Prof. Yinyu Ye.

  • Topics Related with Online Resource Allocation: Horizon Uncertainty and Dynamic Competition. with Prof. Simai He.
